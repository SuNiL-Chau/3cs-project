About Us

Accelerate your Supply Chain Success with Al/ML

3SC offers businesses intelligent & comprehensive supply chain solutions providing them with scalability, visibility & improved collaboration amongst supply chain stakeholders & partners. Driven by Al & ML, our supply chain analytics (SCA) & supply chain execution (SCE) platforms enhance the customer experience by tapping into potential market opportunities.

The 3SC Mission

Creating a better world by enabling businesses use supply chain analytics to become sustainable and profitable.

The 3SC Vision

3SC is a data, analytics and intelligence partner of choice for our customers. A partner our customers rely on to get actionable intelligence in an increasingly unpredictable world
